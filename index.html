I'm a Product Designer dedicated to bringing clarity and elegance to complex digital systems. With deep roots in SaaS, Fintech, and Healthcare, I thrive where logic meets ambiguity, transforming heavy data into intuitive, actionable experiences. For me, design is about more than screens: it's about Data Storytelling, bridging the gap between business complexity and the human need for control.

My fascination with design has always been a fascination with people, how we think, navigate, and feel. This curiosity follows me outside of Figma too. When I'm not architecting workflows, you'll find me painting, experimenting with digital illustrations, or analyzing UX patterns in game streams. And when I need to step away from pixels, you can find me on the tennis court 🎾
